📐 Formel-Dreiecke zum Nachschauen

Masse, Stoffmenge & molare Masse

m = Masse (in g)
n = Stoffmenge (in mol)
M = molare Masse (in g/mol)

m = n · M
n = m / M
M = m / n

Volumen, Stoffmenge & molares Volumen

V = Volumen (in L)
n = Stoffmenge (in mol)
Vm = molares Volumen (in L/mol)

V = n · Vm
n = V / Vm
Vm = V / n
